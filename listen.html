On Cherry Red Records

Released at Last

"We waited 38 years to hear this. Now is your opportunity to relive the sounds of Southbound." Recorded in the 1970s with producer Steve Thompson and finally released in August 2015 on Cherry Red Records — the songs George Lamb and Alan Burke wrote, out in the world at last.

Just two weeks after these were released, our joint lead guitarist, singer and songwriter — and all-round great guy — Alan Burke passed away. But at least he got to hear the songs he co-wrote with George Lamb.

The Bedrock Festival Album

Southbound recorded a track for the Bedrock Festival album "All Together" at Impulse Studios, Wallsend. Also on the record: Kip, Moonlight Drive, Scratchband, Sidekick, Michael Ford's Limousine, Young Bucks, Hot Snax, East Coast, Steve Brown Band and the Junco Partners.
