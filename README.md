# A Musical History by Mick Kelly — Southbound

A rebuild of Mick Kelly's music-history website, telling the story of the North East of England band **Southbound** (1970s–80s) alongside newspaper clippings, photographs, record-company letters and the *Released at Last* album.

## Pages
- **index.html** — home
- **story.html** — the memoir
- **southbound.html** — the band scrapbook (photo gallery)
- **bands.html** — "Bands We've Seen" concert photos
- **media.html** — clippings, record-company letters & every venue played
- **listen.html** — the album, streaming links and 1977 video

## Tech
Plain static HTML/CSS/JS — no build step. Just open `index.html`, or host the folder anywhere. All images live in `images/`.
